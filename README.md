# ip1-annikabergstrom
Builds a dice game that
a. Has 2 human players controlled by the command line who alternate turns
b. On each player's turn, one six-sided die is rolled for them 
c. The player can choose to re-roll the die up to 2 times
d. Once the player chooses to end their turn, the value of the die is added to their score
e. Each player shpould get 10 turns
f. At the edn of the game, the player with the most points wins
